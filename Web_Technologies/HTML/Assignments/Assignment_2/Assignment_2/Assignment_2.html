How to Make Coffee: 6 Steps

Step 1: Gather Ingredients
Get coffee grounds, fresh water, and any other ingredients like milk or sugar.
Step 2: Boil Water
Heat water until it reaches boiling point (about 100°C or 212°F).
Step 3: Prepare Coffee Maker
Place the coffee grounds into your coffee maker or filter.
Step 4: Pour Hot Water
Slowly pour the hot water over the coffee grounds, allowing the coffee to brew.
Step 5: Let it Brew
Allow the coffee to steep for about 3-5 minutes depending on desired strength.
Step 6: Serve and Enjoy
Pour the brewed coffee into a cup and add milk or sugar if desired. Enjoy!

Subhas Chandra Bose

Subhas Chandra Bose was a prominent leader in the Indian independence movement against British rule. Known for his militant approach and leadership of the Indian National Army (INA), he remains an iconic figure in India’s fight for freedom.

Achievements

  • Founded the Indian National Army (INA) to fight against British colonial rule.
  • Served as the President of the Indian National Congress in 1938 and 1939.
  • Advocated for armed resistance and international alliances to gain India's independence.
  • Popularized the slogan "Give me blood, and I shall give you freedom!"
  • Played a key role in inspiring patriotism and the freedom movement across India.

Bose's vision and determination made him one of the most influential and respected leaders in Indian history.
